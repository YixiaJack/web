How the assignment works

Your title and summary are hashed (FNV-1a 32-bit, see js/demo.js). The hash indexes into a real catalog of NASA celestial bodies in data/bodies.js, drawn from the Exoplanet Archive, the JPL Small-Body Database, and confirmed solar-system moons and comets. Same idea, same body — forever.

In the full architecture, body assignment uses a Blake2b hash of the idea's embedding plus a category lottery weighted 70/25/4/1 across asteroid / exoplanet / comet / moon. The demo uses a simpler hash so everything runs in your browser, but the principle is the same.
